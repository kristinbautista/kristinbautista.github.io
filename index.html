What I Do

I specialize in Interaction Design, aiming to create meaningful connections between people and the world around them. My work includes marketing design, graphic design for cultural events, and product design for nonprofits.

Next

Who I Am

I'm a student at the University of Washington, pursuing a Bachelor of Design in Interaction Design with a minor in Architectural Studies. My journey in design is fueled by a passion for improving conditions for underserved communities and exploring the intersection of technology and public safety.

My Work

Below are some of my projects that showcase my skills and creativity. Click on the images to learn more about each project.

Retrospect

An AR video game that connects young adults and their parents with their ancestral history using AI and AR technology to create a personalized learning experience.

Art Blox

A wooden toy puzzle made of 9 blocks depicting 6 different paintings of love. The player discovers a painting created when the blocks are arranged correctly.

Nostalgia

An original LGBTQ musical written, set designed, and directed during senior year. The first use of projector lights in Dramafest history.

Prepare for Arrival

A promotional poster for Alpha Theta Delta’s Summer 2024 Rush, inspired by a space theme. Collaborated with the Creative Director and Professional Development Chair.

Space Adventure

A birthday poster inspired by road trips in a Subaru named “RGB” and the retro posters in a friend's room, expressing love for adventures.

Now Hiring

A magazine cover for the New York Times' Future of Work Issue, featuring two contrasting covers to evoke different tones of the articles assigned.
